Welcome to

Nordlander

Nordlander is an open world action rpg where you shape the world's future!

The base game includes the base world of "Grim", a dark fantasy world based off brutal norse mythology, where players can:

  • Fight using an array of weapons, from swords and bows to guns and spells
  • Fully customisable characters
  • Hours of quests and dungeons

But this is just the beginning!
You can shape the world and your playstyle by purchasing additional "features" that can drastically change how you can play!

There are 3 main types of features you can add to your game

  • New worlds
  • New character abilities/weapons/items
  • New global quests

Download the Nordlander base game below!

Click on the correct download icon for your machine, click on the downloaded file and following installation instructions to install.
